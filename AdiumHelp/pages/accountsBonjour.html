Creating a Bonjour Account

Bonjour is a special service available only to users connected to the same Local Area Network that does not require a server or registering an account.

To Create a Bonjour Account:

  1. Choose Preferences from the Adium menu.
  2. Click the Accounts button at the top.
  3. Click the + button.
  4. Choose Bonjour from the menu.
  5. Enter your name in the Account panel.
  6. Enter your alias and choose an icon in the Personal panel
  7. Choose whether you would like to send typing notices in the Privacy panel.
  8. Click OK.
