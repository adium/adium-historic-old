Customizing the Interface

Adium's user experience is easily modified though the use of add-ons called Xtras. A wide variety of Xtras can be found on the AdiumXtras website.

If you are the do-it-yourself type you can also create your own Xtras and submit them to the AdiumXtras website for the rest of the Adium community to access.

Adium now includes an Xtras Manager from which you can easily browse and remove installed Xtras. You access the new Xtras Manager from the Adium menu.

Click here for more detailed information on creating your own Xtras.
