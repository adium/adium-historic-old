Customizing the Interface

Creating a custom Contact List style does not require any programming ability or writing of any code of any kind.
Click here for help getting started making your own custom Contact List.

Creating custom Dock Icons, Emoticons or Status Icons requires no more than a basic understanding of image manipulation; and of course an image editing program such as Photoshop. GIMP is a free alternative to Photoshop for those wishing to create Xtras but who do not own Photoshop.
Click here for more information on making your own Dock Icon.
Click here for more information on making your own Status Icons.

Creating a custom Message Style requires at least a basic knowledge of HTML and Cascading Style Sheets.
Click here for more information on making your own Message Style.

Creating a custom Sound Set or Emoticons requires a basic understanding of audio editing; and a sound editing program. Audacity is a free sound editing program which provides an easy way to get started without needing to buy expensive software.
Click here for more information on making your own Sound Set.



Using the Xtras Manager
Open the Xtras Manager by clicking the Adium menu and choosing Xtras Manager

From here you can see what Xtras are installed. Delete unwanted Xtras by choosing one or more from the list and clicking the Delete button. Click Get More Xtras to go to the AdiumXtras website to find more Xtras.

Changing the Appearance
Choose Preferences from the Adium menu and click the Appearance button at the top.

Changing your Service Icons, Status Icons, Emoticons, Dock Icons is as simple as choosing the one you want from the drop-down menu. From here you can also customize your Contact List.
Click here for help getting started making your own custom Contact List.

Changing the Message Style
Choose Preferences from the Adium menu and click the Messages button at the top.

As you choose different styles from the drop-down menu the preview shows you what that style looks like; many styles also include a set of variants with subtle differences such as color scheme or icon arrangement. User icons, user fonts and chat window background color are also turned on or off from this window.
