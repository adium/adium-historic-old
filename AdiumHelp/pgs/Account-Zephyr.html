Setting up a Zephyr Account

Zephyr is blah blah blah.

To Create a Zephyr Account:

  1. Choose Preferences from the Adium menu.
  2. Click the Accounts button at the top.
  3. Click the + button.
  4. Choose Zephyr from the menu.
  5. Enter your user name in the Account panel.
  6. Enter your alias and choose an icon in the Personal panel
  7. Enter you exposure and encoding preferences; and check the appropriate boxes in the Options panel.
  8. Choose whether you would like to send typing notices in the Privacy panel.
  9. Setup your proxy configuration in the Proxy panel if you need to (if you don't know what proxy configuration details to enter you don't need to enter any).
  10. Click OK.
