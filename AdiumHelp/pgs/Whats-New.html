What's new in Adium X?

Adium X 1.0 represents over a year and a half of development. As such, there are many new features. Here are a few of the major improvements.

Major AIM, ICQ & .Mac Improvements

Working file transfer, direct IM and faster sign on.


Personal Preferences

Globally manage the profile, buddy icon, and display name of all of your accounts.


General Account Improvements

Accounts can now be disabled when not in in use, and friends can now sign on from your Adium without saving their information.


New Xtras Manager

Better handling of Xtras browsing and removal.


Privacy Settings

Powerful per-account and global control over your privacy settings.


Look & Feel Improvements

Updated default look and feel, new built-in Xtras, prettier and more functional regular window contact list.


Redesigned Chat Transcript Viewer (was Log Viewer)

Spotlight integration (10.4 and higher only), Mail-style interface, greatly improved searching speed.


And lots more!
